## First Steps

After installation:

1. **Test the system**: `udos version`
2. **Explore workspace**: `cd ~/uMEMORY/sandbox`
3. **Check documentation**: See `system/core/docs/`

## Key Commands

- `udos` - Main interface
- `uvar` - Variable management  
- `udata` - Data management
- `utpl` - Template management
